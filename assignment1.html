Application of Virtual Simulation Technology in Automobile Manufacturing

Another significent improvement in life protection

Virtual simulation in automotive safety models collisions, uncovers design flaws, and reduces R&D costs significantly.

In automotive engineering, computers are used to create 3D models and simulate scenarios like wind tunnel tests and collisions. This is crucial as it enables engineers to optimize designs, enhance safety, and cut costs by avoiding costly physical prototypes.
